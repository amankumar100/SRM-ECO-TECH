SRM ECO TECH is a technology-driven company focused on delivering innovative IT solutions and sustainable environmental compliance services. With expertise across custom software development, automation, BPM applications, and AI integrations, we help businesses modernize their operations and unlock growth.

Alongside our core IT services, we are also recognized for our trusted EPR (Extended Producer Responsibility) consultancy, supporting companies in meeting regulatory obligations for plastic waste management. At SRM ECO TECH, we combine technical excellence with environmental responsibility to serve industries across India — from startups to large enterprises — with a vision to digitally empower and sustainably transform the future.

As a part of our dynamic management practices, we offer other services like compliance management and reverse logistics. We are actively involved in campaigns and programs to promote holistic management of electronic and plastic wastes.
