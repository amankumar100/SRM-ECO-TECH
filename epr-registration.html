Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) Overview

Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) is a government-mandated environmental policy that holds producers accountable for the entire lifecycle of their products, especially take-back, recycling, and final disposal. In India, EPR registration is mandatory for stakeholders dealing with plastic, e-waste, battery, tyre, and used oil management.

At SRM ECO TECH, we offer a comprehensive suite of EPR registration and compliance services. Our experienced team ensures accurate documentation, smooth portal registration, strategic compliance planning, and timely submissions to the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB). We make complex regulatory processes simple, reliable, and fast — enabling your business to stay compliant and environmentally responsible.

Plastic Waste EPR

  • Mandatory for PIBOs (Producers, Importers & Brand Owners)
  • Registration under CPCB with packaging data
  • Recycling and end-of-life disposal targets
  • Submission of annual reports and recycling certificates

Electronic Waste (E-Waste)

  • For producers, refurbishers & recyclers of electronics
  • EPR registration and compliance through CPCB portal
  • Reverse logistics and collection strategy planning
  • Annual compliance reports and inventory tracking

Battery Waste Management

  • Applicable to lead-acid, lithium, and nickel batteries
  • Battery EPR portal registration and category reporting
  • Setup of collection mechanisms and disposal partners
  • Performance audits and compliance submissions

Used Oil EPR

  • For producers/importers of base oil/lubricants
  • Registration with CPCB and obligation targets
  • Collaboration with certified oil recyclers
  • End-to-end tracking & annual performance reports

Tyre Waste Management

  • Registration required for manufacturers/importers
  • Recycling, co-processing and recovery targets
  • Digital traceability through CPCB platform
  • Annual EPR filings and compliance certification
